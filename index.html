Battle the daily struggle of time.

Your equipment: a goat and some drinks.

find out more

Meet Marvin, a local farmboy in the Highlands, a far-off village where time is just an illusion. This makes the daily ride to school a difficult task. But you can help! Since bikes or cars are not agile enough to weather the outstanding landscapes Marvin has to travel to, you will be traveling by... goat!

The road is full of obstacles you need to jump over! Progress and unlock new worlds as your highscore grows. Eventually you'll get at school. And then you'll understand or recognize that for some people (like Marvin), getting there is a real challenge!
